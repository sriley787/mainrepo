What is the project management professional toolkit & guide?

The professional project management toolkit is a guide for project manager's from all backgrounds, industries, and experience levels. This is a forum to share guides, resources, process flows, charts, and best practices in one location to help project managers at all levels more easily find helpful information. It is focused on IT project management & processes, rather than construction or industrial.
